I’ve been drawn to the world of code and machines since I was a kid, starting with simple batch scripts and diving deeper from there. By freshman year of high school, I was fully immersed in programming—and my fascination with the networked world has only grown since. Now, after years of absorbing concepts, languages, and patterns, I’m focused on building. Taking all the fragments I’ve gathered and turning them into the systems, tools, and experiences I imagined a decade ago—The Nexus is the realization of a vision that’s been with me from the beginning. I hope to be able to expand my skillset through the development of this website, and to document things that I create, discover, or ponder. I hope that you, the observer of this node, can take something and transmute it into something of your own.
